About This Event

This comprehensive workshop series is designed to introduce students to the exciting world of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Whether you're a complete beginner or have some programming experience, this series will provide you with hands-on experience in building and deploying AI models.

The workshop will cover fundamental concepts including supervised learning, unsupervised learning, neural networks, and deep learning. Participants will work with popular frameworks such as TensorFlow and PyTorch, and will complete a capstone project by the end of the series.

No prior AI/ML experience is required, but basic programming knowledge (Python preferred) is recommended. All necessary tools and datasets will be provided during the workshop.
